Vietnamese Pho

Description

Delicious chicken pho made with chicken thighs topped with raw onion slices and coriander.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (preferably chicken thighs)
  • Leftover chicken for broth
  • Cloves, fennel, star anise, coriander seeds and cinnamon
  • Onions
  • Ginger
  • Fish sauce
  • Rock sugar

    • Toppings

  • Lime
  • Chili (optional)
  • Coriander
  • Thai basil
  • Green onion
  • Bean sprouts
  • Rice noodles

Steps

  1. Char the onion, ginger, and spices.
  2. Add everything to the pot.
  3. Simmer for 1.5 hours.
  4. Scoop off the scum at the top of the broth.
  5. Strain it, so you only get the broth.
  6. Prepare noodles just before serving.
  7. Serve with toppings!
