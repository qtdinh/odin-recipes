Garlic-parmesan shrimp

Description

Delicious crispy shrimp coated in a bread crumb-Parmesan mixture.

Ingredients

  • nonstick cooking spray
  • 1 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • 1 and a half teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F. Spray a 9x13-inch glass casserole dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Mix the bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley and garlic powder together in a large resealable plastic bag. Add shrimp and shake to coat.
  3. Lay shrimp flat in the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle with any remaining bread crumb mixture remaining in the bag. Drizzle melted butter over the top.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until it starts to crisp; 15 to 20 minutes. Switch to the broil setting until the topping is light brown; 2 to 3 minutes.
